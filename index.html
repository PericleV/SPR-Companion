Welcome to SPR Companion

Your complete workflow for simulating, analyzing, and optimizing planar multilayer structures and Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) based sensors.

Database & Materials

Define the exact optical properties for the layers in your stack.

  • Material Types: Standard bulk media, 2D materials, and porous effective mediums.
  • Data Import: Constant index (n, k), Tabulated dispersive data (CSV), Analytic models (Drude, Sellmeier), or directly digitize data from literature plots using the built-in Digitizer.
Geometry Design

Construct the physical multilayer stack from your defined materials.

  • Customization: Build arbitrary layer sequences, from simple Kretschmann configurations to complex Distributed Bragg Reflectors (DBRs).
  • Export & Import: Save your entire geometry configuration as a workspace file to easily load and share your designs later.
Setup & Simulation

Run fast, analytical Transfer Matrix Method (TMM) calculations.

  • Interrogation Modes: Perform both Angular (fixed wavelength) and Spectral (fixed angle) interrogations.
  • Analysis: Automatic curve fitting to identify resonance dips and extraction of critical performance parameters (Sensitivity, FWHM, Figure of Merit).
Parameter Sweep

Visualize how structural variations affect the optical response.

  • Vary any geometric or material parameter systematically to observe trends.
  • Generate high-resolution 2D heatmaps and trace dispersion curves to locate optimal coupling regimes.
Optimization

Optimize your structure to achieve the desired optical performance.

  • Algorithms: Single-Objective GA (SOGA), Multi-Objective NSGA-II, Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO), and Gradient Descent with ADAM.
  • Flexibility: Define multiple objectives concurrently, apply strict constraints, combine performance metrics, or fit the structure to a target optical curve.
